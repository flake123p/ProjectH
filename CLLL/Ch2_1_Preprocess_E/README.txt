How to start?
Ans: type "make"

Key Point:
1. [gcc --help] 
	-E          Preprocess only; do not compile, assemble or link
	-o <file>   Place the output into <file>

2. Output file: main.i

3. What items are removed?
	a. #define
	b. #include
	c. comments

4. What item is not removed?
	a. #pragma
	(Compiler needs it.)
